About Us

The Leading Immigration Consultancy in Kenya

Iterin Immigration is a specialized consultancy firm authorized by the Government of Kenya to offer professional immigration services based on the Kenya Immigration Act and Nationality and Citizenship Laws. We have successfully advised and guided international organizations, businesses, and individuals on Kenya's immigration requirements for over five years.

We specialize in delivering seamless, fully compliant immigration solutions — from work permits and passes to citizenship and permanent residency. Our experienced team combines deep regulatory knowledge with genuine care for every client's journey to Kenya.

Whether you're moving to Kenya for work, study, business, or family, our team will handle every step of your immigration journey, making the process simple and stress-free.

Areas of Expert Practice

Expert visa, residency & citizenship support — tailored to your specific needs, every step of the way.

Entry Work Permits

Issued under Section 40 of the Kenya Citizenship and Immigration Act, 2011. We handle all permit classes enabling foreign nationals to enter Kenya for trade, employment, farming, business, missionary activities or retirement.

Visitors Pass Extension

Citizenship

Expert guidance through Kenya's citizenship application process. We assess your eligibility, prepare all required documentation, and manage the full submission to the Directorate of Immigration Services.

Permanent Residency Status

A status granted under the Kenya Citizenship and Immigration Act. We guide you through eligibility requirements, Class M permit applications, and Certificate of Permanent Residence proceedings.

Alien Card / Foreign National Certificate
