Regularization

If your immigration status has lapsed or is irregular, our regularization service brings you back into full legal compliance — discreetly and efficiently.

Regularization

Immigration Status Correction & Compliance

Regularization services involve helping individuals or businesses bring their legal, compliance, or documentation status up to date in line with government regulations. This includes assisting with immigration status corrections, updating expired licenses or permits, resolving tax or statutory compliance issues, formalizing informal businesses, or rectifying discrepancies in official records.

Situations We Handle

  • Expired work permits or passes
  • Overstay resolution
  • Incorrect immigration status
  • Pending penalty resolution
  • Document discrepancies
  • Informal business formalization
  • Statutory compliance backlog
  • Record rectification

Our Approach

As a service provider, we guide clients through all required procedures, preparing and submitting necessary documents, liaising with relevant authorities, and ensuring that all obligations are met, enabling the client to operate or reside legally without facing penalties or interruptions.

Confidentiality

We understand that regularization situations are often sensitive. All matters handled by Iterin Immigration are treated with the strictest confidentiality. Our consultants work discreetly and professionally to resolve your situation without unnecessary exposure.

Why Act Now?

Irregular immigration status attracts increasing penalties over time and can result in arrest, detention, and deportation. The earlier you seek regularization, the more options are available and the lower the cost of resolution. Contact us today for a confidential assessment.
